Semantic Search Engine for Industrial Parts identification

Download Project Poster

Project information

  • Category: Data science, data engineering
  • Techniques: NLP, deployement
  • Sector: Industry
  • Client: Pzartech
  • Project end date: 01 March, 2023

The project aims to address the challenges faced by ICL Group, a multinational manufacturing concern, in identifying spare parts for their maintenance equipment. Due to missing or deteriorated parts, visual recognition through photos is not always possible. To overcome this issue, the project focuses on developing a machine learning model that can match common words or phrases to item descriptions linked to serial numbers. By utilizing a fine-tuned sentence transformer model and leveraging embeddings, the model aims to achieve a minimum accuracy of 70%. The ultimate goal is to significantly reduce the time required for technicians to identify parts and enhance the efficiency of maintenance operations. An API will be developed to process common words and quickly return the best matching item description, enabling easy accessibility for technicians. With this solution, the project seeks to streamline the identification process and improve overall maintenance workflow within ICL Group.
